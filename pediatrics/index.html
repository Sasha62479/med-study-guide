🦟 Tick-Borne Exanthems
Lyme Disease (Borrelia burgdorferi / Ixodes tick)
EM bull's-eye
Erythema migrans expanding ≥5cm from bite
Stage 1: EM, fatigue → Stage 2: facial palsy, carditis → Stage 3: arthritis
Tx: Doxy ≥8yr; amox <8yr; ceftriaxone (neuro/carditis)
RMSF (Rickettsia rickettsii / Dermacentor tick)
Petechial rash
Fever + severe headache
Rash: wrists/ankles → centrally; palms & soles; no rapid test
Tx: Doxy ALL ages — DO NOT DELAY!
⚠️ RMSF: Start doxycycline immediately. Delay = significantly ↑ mortality!

Tuberculosis (M. tuberculosis — airborne)
LTBI: TST/IGRA+, asymptomatic; Tx: INH×9mo or RIF×4mo
Active TB: Cough >3wks, fever, night sweats, weight loss, hemoptysis; CXR: upper lobe ± cavitation

🧠 High-Yield Pearls & Mnemonics

Measles Prodrome
Cough · Coryza · Conjunctivitis + Koplik spots (pathognomonic!)
TORCH
Congenital Infections
Toxoplasma · Other (Syphilis/Parvo) · Rubella · CMV · HSV-2
RIPE
Active TB Induction (2 mo)
Rifampin · Isoniazid · Pyrazinamide · Etambutol → then RI × 4 more months
1–6
Numbered Exanthems
1 Measles · 2 Scarlet Fever · 3 Rubella · 4 Duke's · 5 Parvovirus B19 · 6 Roseola (HHV-6)
Doxy
Tick-Borne Rx
Lyme + RMSF → doxycycline. RMSF: treat empirically NOW — no rapid test available!
⚡ Traps
Don't Miss!
Amox rash + sore throat = EBV (not allergy!) · Parvo NOT contagious once rash appears · Nikolsky+ in child = SSSS (not TEN)
