Physiology

How each body system functions normally — mechanisms, feedback loops & clinical correlations

Liver
Metabolism, detoxification, bile synthesis, protein production, and glucose homeostasis.
Gallbladder
Bile concentration, CCK-mediated contraction, sphincter of Oddi regulation.
Pancreas
Enzyme secretion, insulin and glucagon regulation, glucose sensing mechanisms.
Kidney
GFR, tubular reabsorption and secretion, acid-base, fluid and electrolyte balance.
Heart
Cardiac cycle, Frank-Starling, action potentials, autonomic regulation of HR and CO.
Lungs
Ventilation, perfusion, gas exchange, lung volumes, and respiratory control.
Neurological
Action potentials, neurotransmitters, ANS, reflexes, and sensory/motor pathways.
GU / Reproductive
Micturition reflex, menstrual cycle, hormonal feedback axes, and fertilization.
Integumentary
Thermoregulation, barrier function, sensory transduction, and vitamin D synthesis.
