Pathophysiology

Disease mechanisms, clinical presentations & diagnostic criteria — organized by body system

Liver
Cirrhosis, hepatitis, NAFLD, portal hypertension, hepatic encephalopathy.
Gallbladder
Cholelithiasis, cholecystitis, choledocholithiasis, ascending cholangitis.
Pancreas
Acute/chronic pancreatitis, diabetes mellitus, pancreatic cancer.
Kidney
AKI, CKD staging, nephrotic/nephritic syndromes, glomerulonephritis.
Heart
HFrEF/HFpEF, ACS (STEMI/NSTEMI/UA), arrhythmias, valvular disease.
Lungs
Asthma, COPD, pneumonia, pulmonary embolism, pneumothorax, ARDS.
Neuro
Stroke, TIA, seizures, meningitis, Parkinson's, Alzheimer's, MS.
GU / Repro
UTI, BPH, PCOS, endometriosis, STIs, preeclampsia, ectopic pregnancy.
Skin
Psoriasis, eczema, cellulitis, melanoma, BCC, SCC, acne, tinea.
