Anatomy

Gross, microscopic, embryology & clinical correlates — organ by organ

Liver

Hepatic lobules, bile production, portal circulation, and metabolic functions.

Gallbladder

Bile storage and concentration, cystic duct anatomy, and cholelithiasis risk.

Pancreas

Exocrine and endocrine anatomy, islets of Langerhans, and ductal system.

Kidney

Nephron structure, renal cortex/medulla, vascular supply, and JGA.

Heart

Cardiac chambers, valves, conduction system, coronary arteries, and pericardium.

Lungs

Bronchopulmonary segments, pleura, alveolar structure, and pulmonary vasculature.

Neurological

Cerebral lobes, brainstem, spinal cord tracts, cranial nerves, and cerebral blood supply.

GU / Reproductive

Urinary tract, male and female reproductive anatomy, hormones, and embryology.

Integumentary

Skin layers, epidermal cells, appendages, sensory receptors, and wound healing.

Pharmacology

Drug classes, mechanisms, side effects & contraindications — organized by body system

Liver

Antivirals (DAAs, tenofovir), lactulose, rifaximin, hepatotoxic drugs, ursodiol.

Gallbladder

Ursodiol, cholestyramine, antibiotics for biliary infections, antispasmodics.

Pancreas

Insulin types, metformin, GLP-1 agonists, SGLT-2 inhibitors, DPP-4 inhibitors, pancreatic enzymes.

Kidney

Diuretics, ACE-I/ARBs, SGLT-2 inhibitors, phosphate binders, ESAs, patiromer.

Heart

Beta-blockers, ACE-I/ARNi, statins, antiarrhythmics, anticoagulants, antiplatelets.

Lungs

SABAs, LABAs, LAMAs, ICS, biologics (omalizumab), antibiotics, mucolytics.

Neuro

AEDs, levodopa/carbidopa, tPA, MS disease-modifying therapies, dementia drugs.

GU / Repro

OCPs, spironolactone, GnRH agonists, UTI antibiotics, oxytocin, magnesium.

Skin

Topical steroids, retinoids, isotretinoin, biologics (dupilumab, adalimumab), antifungals.
