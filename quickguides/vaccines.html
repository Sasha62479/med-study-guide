🚫 Contraindications & Special Populations
  • Live vaccines (MMR, Varicella, LAIV, Zoster live): Contraindicated in pregnancy and severe immunocompromise
  • Egg allergy: Flu vaccine can be given to all including egg allergy (use non-egg or egg-based with precautions)
  • Immunocompromised: Avoid live vaccines; may need extra doses of inactivated vaccines
  • Pregnancy: Safe: Flu (IIV), Tdap, COVID-19, HepA, HepB, Pneumococcal (if indicated)
  • Asplenia: PCV, PPSV23, MenACWY, MenB, Hib all indicated
  • HIV: CD4 >200: MMR and Varicella may be given; avoid if CD4 <200
Catch-up tip: Use CDC's Immunization Schedule tool at cdc.gov/vaccines for patient-specific catch-up schedules. The minimum intervals between doses are critical to remember for exams.
