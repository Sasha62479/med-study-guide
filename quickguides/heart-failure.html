💧 Diuresis & Decongestion
  • Loop diuretics: Furosemide (first-line), Bumetanide, Torsemide (better bioavailability)
  • Goal: Euvolemia - assess JVD, peripheral edema, orthopnea, daily weights
  • Restrict fluids: 1.5-2L/day if severe; sodium <2g/day
  • Daily weight: Gain of >2 lbs overnight or >5 lbs in week = call provider
  • Thiazides: Add metolazone for diuretic resistance (powerful combination)
  • Monitor: BMP for electrolytes and renal function during diuresis
🚨 Acute Decompensated HF (ADHF)
  • IV loop diuretics (furosemide) - 2.5x oral dose IV
  • Supplemental O2; non-invasive ventilation (BiPAP) if needed
  • Vasodilators (nitroglycerin, nesiritide) if adequate BP
  • Avoid hypotension; careful with ACE-I/ARB in acute phase
  • Continue GDMT unless hemodynamically unstable
  • Inotropes (dobutamine, milrinone) for cardiogenic shock
Remember LMNOP: Lasix (diuresis), Morphine (anxiolysis, vasodilation), Nitrates (preload reduction), Oxygen, Position (upright/Fowler's)
