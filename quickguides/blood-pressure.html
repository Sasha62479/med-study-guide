🌿 Lifestyle Modifications (DASH)
  • Weight loss: 5-20 mmHg reduction per 10 kg lost
  • DASH diet: Fruits, vegetables, low-fat dairy; reduce saturated fat
  • Sodium: Reduce to <2.4g/day (ideally <1.5g)
  • Physical activity: 90-150 min aerobic/week (3-5 mmHg reduction)
  • Limit alcohol: ≤2 drinks/day men; ≤1 drink/day women
  • Smoking cessation: Critical for overall CV risk
  • Stress reduction: Meditation, yoga, counseling

🚨 Hypertensive Crisis
Hypertensive Urgency (no organ damage): BP ≥180/120 but asymptomatic. Oral agents (clonidine, labetalol). Lower BP over 24-48 hrs. Outpatient management often appropriate.
Hypertensive Emergency (with organ damage): Same BP + end-organ damage (AKI, encephalopathy, pulmonary edema, MI, aortic dissection, eclampsia). IV medications (nicardipine, labetalol, nitroprusside). Reduce MAP by 10-20% in first hour, then 25% over 24 hrs. ICU admission.
