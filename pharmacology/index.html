Pharmacology

Drug classes, mechanisms, side effects & contraindications — organized by body system

Liver
Antivirals (DAAs, tenofovir), lactulose, rifaximin, hepatotoxic drugs, ursodiol.
Gallbladder
Ursodiol, cholestyramine, antibiotics for biliary infections, antispasmodics.
Pancreas
Insulin types, metformin, GLP-1 agonists, SGLT-2 inhibitors, DPP-4 inhibitors, pancreatic enzymes.
Kidney
Diuretics, ACE-I/ARBs, SGLT-2 inhibitors, phosphate binders, ESAs, patiromer.
Heart
Beta-blockers, ACE-I/ARNi, statins, antiarrhythmics, anticoagulants, antiplatelets.
Lungs
SABAs, LABAs, LAMAs, ICS, biologics (omalizumab), antibiotics, mucolytics.
Neuro
AEDs, levodopa/carbidopa, tPA, MS disease-modifying therapies, dementia drugs.
GU / Repro
OCPs, spironolactone, GnRH agonists, UTI antibiotics, oxytocin, magnesium.
Skin
Topical steroids, retinoids, isotretinoin, biologics (dupilumab, adalimumab), antifungals.
